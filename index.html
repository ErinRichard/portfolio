LEARNING TO CODE!

A little about me and why I'm learning to code, March 2021

I've recently decided to learn how to program, starting with Python. To assist with my new endeavor, I have chosen to attend Coding Temple's Python Web Development Bootcamp. Prior to this transition, I worked for over 16 years in an administrative role in an office setting for a non-profit training organization. Throughout the course of my employment, I "wore many hats", including reception, document production, office support, accounting, and managing a survey line of business. There were many times that projects or needs arose when it would have been helpful to have someone to go to for assistance with web development and data processing and interpretation. I hope to learn some of those valuable skills through the Coding Temple course. I also hope that Python will be a good supplement to my existing background and will allow me to help organizations improve their online resources and make informed business decisions using real data.

When I'm not busy learning Python, I enjoy spending time with my family, running, and being outside!
